Source: Official exam policy (syllabus appendix)
Course: CS 482 Applied Algorithms

All exam answers must include:
1. Algorithm description or pseudocode
2. Correctness justification (brief proof or invariant argument)
3. Time complexity analysis
4. Space complexity analysis when non-trivial

Points may be deducted if only the final numeric answer is provided.

Regrade requests must be submitted within one week of grade release with written justification.
