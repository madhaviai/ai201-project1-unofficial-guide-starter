Source: Student Forum compilation
Course: CS 482
Focus: Greedy vs DP decision problems

Topics Covered:
- Activity selection (greedy works)
- Weighted interval scheduling (DP required)
- Coin change (depends on coin set)

Most difficult question:
Explain why greedy fails with specific counterexample.

Common mistake:
Asserting greedy works without proof.

Exam tip:
If problem has weights or dependencies between choices, suspect DP first.
