Source: Unofficial graph cram sheet
Course: CS 482
Focus: Graph algorithm exams

Topics Covered:
- BFS shortest paths (unweighted)
- Dijkstra (non-negative edges)
- Bellman-Ford (negative edges allowed)
- Complexity O(V+E) vs O(E log V)

Most difficult question type:
Pick correct algorithm and justify choice.

Common mistake:
Running Dijkstra when negative edges present.

Average points lost on this mistake: 8-12 per exam.
